State Capital: Phoenix & The Surrounding Valley

Photo by Ian Dziuk on Unsplash

The Phoenix Valley is one of the best places to live in the U.S. The main city, Phoenix which is also the state capital, is one of the top 15 largest cities in the states. Multiple division one colleges, satellite campuses, and community colleges are dispersed throughout, providing a diverse population of talented and intriguing individuals. The growing economy continues as more companies chose the valley for headquarters, and manufacturing locations. Don't worry small business owners, there is plenty of growth and opportunity for you as well, with skilled worked at the ready.

Top Places to Visit in Phoenix/Phoenix Valley:

  1. Downtown Phoenix at night
  2. Old Town Scottsdale during the day
  3. Camelback Mountian for an outside 360 degree view
