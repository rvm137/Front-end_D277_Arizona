Sedona Valley

Photo by Edmundo Mendez, Jr. on Unsplash

Sedona Valley (Sedona), is an outdoor haven for everyone from the novice explorer, to seasoned pro. With ample amount of routes for hiking, biking and offroading you are sure to find a spot where time slips away and you just emjoy the view. Temperatures still tend to soar during one to two months during the summer, but for the rest of the year the weather is perfect. Also home to a an eclectic group of artist, you will be sure to find keep sakes both big and small. ADVENTURE AWAITS!

Top Places to Visit in Sedona:

  1. Downtown Art Scene on the weekends
  2. Devil's Bridge
  3. Subway Cave
